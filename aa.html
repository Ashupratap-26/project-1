About Programming and Tech Skill Club (PTSC)

The Programming and Tech Skill Club (PTSC) is a student-driven initiative aimed at nurturing and expanding the technical capabilities of aspiring developers, engineers, and innovators.

Our mission is to provide a collaborative environment where learners of all levels can engage with technology through workshops, seminars, real-world projects, coding challenges, and group discussions. Whether you're diving into your first line of code or building advanced software systems, PTSC is your launchpad for growth.

At PTSC, we believe that learning should be hands-on and meaningful. Members participate in open-source contributions, prepare for national and international tech events, and develop impactful software solutions. We focus on areas like Web Development, AI/ML, Cybersecurity, Cloud Computing, Blockchain, and Competitive Programming.

With a vision to create tech leaders of tomorrow, PTSC continuously pushes boundaries, encourages peer learning, and celebrates innovation in every form. We are not just a club ‚Äî we are a movement of passionate minds united by a love for technology.
